================================================================================
Bright Money — One-tap onboarding to cash-flow underwriting (Plaid Layer + Plaid Consumer Report: CRA Income Insights + CRA Cash Flow Insights)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Bright Money** — consumer fintech offering credit-builder loans / personal lending
**Brand URL:** https://www.brightmoney.co

**User journey (one sentence):**
A new Bright Money applicant starts a credit-builder loan and taps once to onboard with Plaid Layer — a single session that both shares their prefilled identity and permissions their bank — and Bright Money then generates a Plaid Consumer Report (CRA) server-side to underwrite on verified income and cash-flow behavior, approving the loan in minutes.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Aaliyah Brooks wants a $1,500 credit-builder loan. Instead of a long signup form, Bright Money leads with Plaid Layer: in one tap she reviews her prefilled name, email, phone, and address AND permissions her bank — a single Layer session, no second connection step. Bright Money writes that identity to the Plaid user record and generates her Consumer Report server-side. Moments later it reads **CRA Income Insights** (verified biweekly payroll, monthly net income, predicted next payday) and **CRA Cash Flow Insights** (model-ready attributes: low income volatility, NSF history, discretionary-vs-essential spend) and approves the loan with terms timed to her income. Compliance-safe language only ("can help," "typically," "up to").

**Product-specific reminders:**
- ONE launch only (Layer). The report-generation beat (step 3) is a server-side async state, NOT a `plaidPhase` launch.
- Layer identity is user-permissioned; it is written to the Plaid user record via `/user/update` before report generation. Frame as "shared / permissioned," not document-verified KYC.
- CRA reports retrieved by `user_id`; `cra_cashflow_insights` requires `cra_base_report` server-side.
- Compliance-safe claims only: "can help," "typically," "up to"; never "guarantee" / "eliminate." Avoid GTM percentage stats in narration.
- Happy path only — no declines/errors.

**Compliance / user data:** FCRA consumer-report consent + permissible purpose (`EXTENSION_OF_CREDIT`) collected in the Layer/CRA session; representative sandbox data.
