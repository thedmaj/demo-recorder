CLARITYPAY + PLAID AUTH — INSTANT BANK VERIFICATION FOR DIGITAL LENDING PAYMENTS
================================================================================

**Host:** **ClarityPay** — digital lending / BNPL-style financing
**Canonical URL:** https://claritypay.com/
**Brand URL:** https://claritypay.com/

**User journey (one sentence):**
An applicant finances a $1,200 purchase with ClarityPay and connects her checking
account through Plaid Link so her account is verified instantly — before her first
installment payment is ever scheduled.

**Story arc (short prose):**
ClarityPay loses applicants when bank verification takes days — micro-deposits mean
drop-off before the first payment is ever collected. Plaid enters at checkout: the
applicant taps one button, connects her bank in a live Plaid Link session, and Plaid
Auth returns verified account and routing numbers in seconds. ClarityPay confirms a
depository checking account is on file, unlocks the payment schedule, and the
applicant finishes setup in a single sitting — Link and fund in seconds, not days.
