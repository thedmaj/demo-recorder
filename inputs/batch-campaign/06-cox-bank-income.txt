**User journey (one sentence):**
A dealer finance manager verifies a buyer's income by having them link their checking
account through Plaid Link — replacing paystub uploads — and Bank Income returns
verified income streams that clear the buyer for auto financing in seconds.

**Story arc (short prose):**
At the dealership finance desk, verifying a buyer's income usually means chasing
paystubs, W-2s, or bank statements — slow, fraud-prone, and a drop-off point in the
deal. The finance manager offers the buyer a faster path: connect your bank securely
with Plaid. The buyer taps Link your bank on the pre-Link screen, completes Plaid Link,
and Bank Income returns verified income — primary employer, monthly income, and a
consistent deposit history. The finance desk sees confirmed income and clears the buyer
for financing, closing the deal without a single document upload. Close on faster
funding decisions, fewer stips, and less income-fraud exposure.
