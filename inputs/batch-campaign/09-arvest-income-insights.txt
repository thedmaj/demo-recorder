Arvest Bank — CRA Income Insights: verify income with one bank connection
================================================================================

**Host:** **Arvest Bank** — regional bank / consumer lending
**Canonical URL:** https://www.arvest.com/
**Brand URL:** https://www.arvest.com/

**Opportunity context:** `Arvest Bank | CBA Live | VOI` (`006UV00000ZLd75YAD`) —
Discovery; pain: pay-stub uploads stall personal-loan applications and force manual review.

**User journey (one sentence):**
A personal-loan applicant at Arvest Bank verifies her income through one Plaid bank
connection instead of uploading pay stubs, and Arvest reads CRA Income Insights from
her Plaid Consumer Report to verify income and approve the loan.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Dana applies for a $15,000 personal loan at Arvest. Instead of hunting down pay stubs,
the application offers bank-based income verification with clear FCRA consent. She taps
Connect your bank and completes the real CRA Plaid Link flow; Arvest requests her
Consumer Report. After a short report-generating beat, Income Insights reveal a verified
employer, biweekly pay frequency, steady monthly net income, and a predicted next
payday. Arvest approves on that verified income — minutes, not days, no documents.
