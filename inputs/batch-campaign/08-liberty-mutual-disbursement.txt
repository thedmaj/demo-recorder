Liberty Mutual — Instant Claims Disbursement with Plaid Transfer
================================================================================

**Host:** **Liberty Mutual** — U.S. property & casualty insurance
**Canonical URL:** https://www.libertymutual.com/
**Brand URL** (for `brand-extract`): https://www.libertymutual.com/

**User journey (one sentence):**
An approved auto-claim policyholder links her bank account in seconds, Liberty Mutual verifies the account and authorizes the payout through Plaid Transfer, and her $4,820 claim lands instantly over RTP instead of waiting a week for a check.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Erica's claim is approved, but the default payout is a mailed check — days of waiting at the worst possible moment. She chooses direct deposit and taps Link your bank. Inside Plaid Link she authenticates with First Platypus Bank and selects her checking account. Plaid Instant Auth verifies the account and routing details instantly — no micro-deposits, no typed account numbers. Plaid Transfer authorizes the credit — risk and compliance checks pass inside the authorization, decision approved — and pays the $4,820 claim over RTP. Erica sees "Claim paid" while the demo is still running. A closing slide quantifies fewer misdirected payouts and a claims experience that builds loyalty at the moment of truth.

**Product-specific reminders:**
- **Transfer**: endpoints verbatim — `POST /transfer/authorization/create`, `POST /transfer/create`; never `/transfer/initiate`. Risk checks run INSIDE the authorization — no separate `/signal/evaluate` panel. Happy path: `decision: "approved"` with `decision_rationale: null`.
- **Credit payout semantics**: `type: "credit"`, `user_present: false`, `credit_funds_source: "ledger"`; end on `transfer.status: settled` (RTP settles in seconds).
- **RTP is Limited Availability** — label the RTP panel "Limited Availability"; `/transfer/capabilities/get` precedes the RTP authorization (fallback to same-day ACH if unsupported).
- **Link**: beat 1 narration ends on the `Link your bank` tap; beat 2 narrates inside the modal only. `products[]` comes from `linkTokenCreate.suggestedClientRequest` — do not hardcode.

**Compliance / user data (one line):**
Claimant-consented bank link for payout only; credit disbursement (no NACHA debit POA needed); US/USD only; no error or declined states in the main demo.
