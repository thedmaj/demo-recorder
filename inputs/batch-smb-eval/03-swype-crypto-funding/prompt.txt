Swype — Fund a stablecoin wallet from any US bank (Identity Verification + Plaid Auth + Identity Match)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Swype** — crypto / blockchain-to-fiat wallet (SMB; stablecoin funding)
**Brand URL:** https://www.swype.network

**User journey (one sentence):**
A new Swype user clears KYC with Identity Verification, links a US bank with one Plaid Link, and Swype confirms the linked account belongs to them (Identity Match) and pulls the ACH rails (Plaid Auth) before funding their stablecoin wallet with a verified ACH transfer.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Alex Rivera wants to fund a stablecoin wallet from a US checking account. Swype needs one-time KYC and assurance the funding account is really theirs — without high-friction onboarding. Alex clears Identity Verification for KYC, then links the bank in one Plaid Link connection. Behind the scenes Swype confirms the account name matches Alex (Identity Match) and retrieves account + routing rails (Plaid Auth), then funds the wallet via ACH. Low friction, low fraud, fast funding.
