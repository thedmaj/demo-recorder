================================================================================
CashRepublic — Verify ownership + underwrite on cash flow (Auth + Identity + Balance + CRA Base Report + Income Insights)
================================================================================

**Host:** **CashRepublic** — personal lender (SMB; installment / personal loans)

**User journey (one sentence):**
A borrower applies for a personal loan and connects their bank once through Plaid Link with FCRA-permissible consent, so CashRepublic can confirm account ownership (Auth + Identity), underwrite ability-to-repay from a CRA Base Report and Income Insights, and check a real-time Balance before scheduling ACH repayments.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Marcus Webb applies for a $2,500 personal loan. CashRepublic loses money to bounced payments and thin-file guesswork, so it asks Marcus to connect his bank. One Plaid Link CRA session collects FCRA consent and the connection; Plaid generates the Consumer Report asynchronously. CashRepublic reads the CRA Base Report (365-day cash flow, zero NSFs) and Income Insights (verified income, next payday), confirms the account belongs to Marcus (Auth + Identity), and checks a real-time Balance before the first debit. The loan is approved with a repayment schedule timed to income. Compliance-safe language only.
