================================================================================
Gringo Coin — Lower-risk cross-border payments to Brazil (Identity Verification + Plaid Auth + Plaid Signal)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Gringo Coin** — cross-border / P2P-international payments (SMB; US → Brazil)

**User journey (one sentence):**
A US sender clears Identity Verification, links a bank with one Plaid Link, and Gringo Coin pulls the ACH rails (Plaid Auth) and risk-scores the funding debit with Plaid Signal before releasing a cross-border payment to Brazil.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Daniela Costa wants to send money from her US bank to family in Brazil. Gringo Coin needs to know the sender is real and that the ACH pull won't bounce or be fraudulent. Daniela clears Identity Verification for KYC, then links her bank in one Plaid Link connection. Gringo Coin retrieves verified account/routing (Plaid Auth) and runs Plaid Signal on the specific debit — a low return-risk score returns ACCEPT, so the payment to Brazil clears without holds.

**Product-specific reminders:**

- Identity Verification + Plaid Signal are host beats here (not live Link launches); each is named in narration.
- Plaid Auth account mask/institution from Link `onSuccess` metadata only.
- Happy path only.
