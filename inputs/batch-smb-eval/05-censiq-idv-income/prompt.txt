================================================================================
Censiq — Verify candidates during hiring (Plaid IDV + Bank Income)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Censiq** — AI-native hiring / onboarding / compliance platform (SMB; B2B HR tech)
**Brand URL:** https://www.censiq.com

**User journey (one sentence):**
During onboarding, a job candidate verifies their identity with Plaid Identity Verification (document + selfie) and confirms their income history with Plaid Bank Income, giving the employer a verified candidate profile without manual document collection.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Priya Nair has accepted an offer and needs to clear pre-hire verification on Censiq. Instead of emailing IDs and pay stubs, Priya completes a Plaid IDV session (document + data source + selfie) to confirm identity, then connects a bank so Plaid Bank Income surfaces a verified employer and income history. Censiq presents the hiring team a verified candidate profile in minutes, cutting fraud and onboarding friction.
