Censiq — Verify candidates during hiring (Identity Verification + Bank Income)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Censiq** — AI-native hiring / onboarding / compliance platform (SMB; B2B HR tech)
**Brand URL:** https://www.censiq.com

**User journey (one sentence):**
During onboarding, a job candidate completes a real Plaid Identity Verification session (document + selfie), then connects a bank in a second real Plaid Link session so Bank Income surfaces a verified employer and income history — giving the employer a verified candidate profile without manual document collection.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Priya Nair has accepted an offer and needs to clear pre-hire verification on Censiq. Instead of emailing IDs and pay stubs, Priya first completes a real Plaid Identity Verification session to confirm identity, then opens a second real Plaid Link session so Bank Income surfaces a verified employer and income history with recent payroll deposits. Censiq presents the hiring team a verified candidate profile in minutes, cutting fraud and onboarding friction.
