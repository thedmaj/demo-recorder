================================================================================
Debit Technologies — Real-time BNPL underwriting (Plaid IDV + Auth + Balance)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Debit Technologies** — payments/BNPL lending (SMB; card processor launching a BNPL division)

**User journey (one sentence):**
A shopper splitting a purchase into a buy-now-pay-later plan verifies their identity with Plaid Identity Verification (KYC), then connects their bank with Plaid Link so Debit Technologies can confirm account ownership (Auth) and check available balance (Balance) to approve the plan in real time.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Jordan Blake wants to split a $1,200 purchase into four payments. Debit Technologies needs to know who Jordan is and whether the funding account is real and good for the payments — without slow manual review. First Jordan completes a Plaid IDV session (document + data source + selfie) for KYC. Then a single Plaid Link connection returns verified account/routing numbers (Auth) and a real-time available balance (Balance). With identity confirmed and funds verified, the four-payment plan is approved on the spot.
