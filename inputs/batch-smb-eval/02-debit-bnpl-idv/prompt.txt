================================================================================
Debit Technologies — Real-time BNPL underwriting (Identity Verification + Plaid Auth + Balance)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Debit Technologies** — payments/BNPL lending (SMB; card processor launching a BNPL division)

**User journey (one sentence):**
A shopper splitting a purchase into a buy-now-pay-later plan clears KYC with Identity Verification, then connects their bank with one Plaid Link so Debit Technologies can confirm account ownership (Plaid Auth) and check available balance before approving the plan in real time.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Jordan Blake wants to split a $1,199 purchase into four payments. Debit Technologies needs to know who Jordan is and whether the funding account is real and good for the payments — without slow manual review. Jordan first clears Identity Verification (document + selfie) for KYC, then makes one Plaid Link connection that returns verified account/routing numbers (Plaid Auth) and a real-time available balance. With identity confirmed and funds verified, the four-payment plan is approved on the spot.
