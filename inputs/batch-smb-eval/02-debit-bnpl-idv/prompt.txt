================================================================================
Debit Technologies — Real-time BNPL underwriting (Identity Verification + Plaid Auth + Balance)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Debit Technologies** — payments/BNPL lending (SMB; card processor launching a BNPL division)

**User journey (one sentence):**
A shopper splitting a purchase into a buy-now-pay-later plan completes a real Plaid Identity Verification session for KYC, then connects their bank in a second real Plaid Link session so Debit Technologies can confirm account ownership (Plaid Auth) and check available balance before approving the plan in real time.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Jordan Blake wants to split a $1,199 purchase into four payments. Debit Technologies needs to know who Jordan is and whether the funding account is real and good for the payments — without slow manual review. Jordan first completes a real Plaid Identity Verification session (document + selfie) for KYC, then opens a second real Plaid Link session that returns verified account/routing numbers (Plaid Auth) and a real-time available balance. With identity confirmed and funds verified, the four-payment plan is approved on the spot.
