================================================================================
blitzo — Fast onboarding for a crypto savings app (Plaid Layer + Identity Verification + Plaid Auth + Balance)
================================================================================

**Host:** **blitzo** — Gen-Z crypto savings / financial-management app (SMB)
**Brand URL:** https://www.blitzo.xyz

**User journey (one sentence):**
A new blitzo user breezes through sign-up with Plaid Layer (network prefill), clears KYC with Identity Verification, then connects a bank with one Plaid Link so blitzo can confirm the account (Plaid Auth) and check balance before funding their first crypto savings deposit.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Sam Okafor is signing up for blitzo and wants to start saving immediately. Instead of a long form, Plaid Layer recognizes Sam from the Plaid network and prefills their details in one tap. blitzo then clears Identity Verification (document + selfie) for its crypto on-ramp KYC. With identity confirmed, one Plaid Link connection verifies the funding account (Plaid Auth) and available balance, and Sam funds a first savings deposit. Onboarding that used to take minutes now takes one tap plus a quick verification.
