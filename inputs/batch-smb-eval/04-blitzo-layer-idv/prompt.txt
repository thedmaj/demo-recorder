================================================================================
blitzo — One-tap onboarding for a crypto savings app (Plaid Layer → IDV → Auth + Balance)
================================================================================

**Host:** **blitzo** — Gen-Z crypto savings / financial-management app (SMB)
**Brand URL:** https://www.blitzo.xyz

**User journey (one sentence):**
A new blitzo user breezes through sign-up with Plaid Layer (network prefill), completes KYC with Plaid Identity Verification, then links a bank with Plaid Link so blitzo can confirm the account (Auth) and check balance (Balance) before funding their first crypto savings deposit.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Sam Okafor is signing up for blitzo and wants to start saving immediately. Instead of a long form, Plaid Layer recognizes Sam from the Plaid network and prefills their details in one tap. blitzo then runs a full Plaid IDV session (document + selfie) to satisfy KYC for its crypto on-ramp. With identity confirmed, a single Plaid Link connection verifies the funding account (Auth) and available balance (Balance), and Sam funds a $100 savings deposit. Onboarding that used to take minutes now takes one tap plus a quick verification.
