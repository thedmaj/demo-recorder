================================================================================
Arro — Cash-flow underwriting for cash advances (Plaid Consumer Report — Base Report + Income Insights)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Arro** — consumer cash-advance / credit-building fintech (SMB lender)
**Brand URL:** https://www.helloarro.com

**User journey (one sentence):**
A returning Arro member requests a small cash advance, connects her bank once through Plaid Link with FCRA-permissible consent, and Arro underwrites her ability to repay from a CRA Base Report (365-day cash flow) and CRA Income Insights (verified income), approving the advance on a schedule aligned to her next payday.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Maya Torres needs a $500 advance to cover a bill before payday. Arro can't lean on a thin bureau file, so it asks her to connect her bank. In one Plaid Link CRA session Arro collects FCRA-permissible consent and the connection; Plaid generates the Consumer Report asynchronously. When it's ready, Arro reads the CRA Base Report (healthy 365-day cash flow, zero NSFs) and CRA Income Insights (verified biweekly payroll, monthly net income, predicted next payday) and approves the advance — repayment timed to her income, not a credit score. Compliance-safe language only ("can help," "typically," "up to").
