================================================================================
Equabli — Friendly digital collections with verified ACH (Plaid Auth + Identity + Balance)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Equabli** — debt collection & recovery platform (SMB; consumer-facing "EQengage" repayment experience)

**User journey (one sentence):**
A consumer resolving a past-due balance on Equabli's EQengage connects their bank with Plaid Link, and Equabli confirms account ownership (Auth + Identity) and checks a real-time Balance before scheduling an affordable recovery ACH payment plan.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Tasha Brooks wants to clear a $1,450 past-due balance without phone calls or surprises. On Equabli's EQengage, she connects her bank in one Plaid Link tap. Equabli verifies the account is hers (Auth + Identity) and checks a real-time Balance so it can schedule a payment she can actually afford — $145/month — debiting only when funds are present. Verified ownership and balance-aware scheduling mean fewer failed payments and a respectful recovery experience.
