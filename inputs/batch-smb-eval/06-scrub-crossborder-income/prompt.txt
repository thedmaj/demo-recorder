================================================================================
Scrub.io — Underwrite diaspora borrowers on US bank data (Identity Verification + Bank Income + Assets)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Scrub.io** — cross-border lending infrastructure (SMB; underwriting data provider)

**User journey (one sentence):**
A diaspora applicant clears Identity Verification, then links a US bank with one Plaid Link so Scrub.io can read verified income (Bank Income) and an assets/balance snapshot to score loan eligibility for a lender in the applicant's home country.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Chidi Okafor lives in the US and is applying for a loan from a Nigerian lender that has no visibility into US financial data. On Scrub.io, Chidi clears Identity Verification for KYC, then connects a US bank. Scrub.io reads verified income (Bank Income) with recent payroll deposits and a snapshot of balances/assets, and produces an eligibility score the home-country lender can trust — turning previously invisible diaspora applicants into underwritable borrowers.
