================================================================================
Scrub.io — Underwrite diaspora borrowers on US bank data (Plaid IDV + Bank Income + Assets)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Scrub.io** — cross-border lending infrastructure (SMB; underwriting data provider)

**User journey (one sentence):**
A diaspora applicant verifies identity with Plaid Identity Verification, then links a US bank so Scrub.io can pull verified income (Bank Income) and assets/balances (Assets) to score loan eligibility for a lender in the applicant's home country.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Chidi Okafor lives in the US and is applying for a loan from a Nigerian lender that has no visibility into US financial data. On Scrub.io, Chidi completes a Plaid IDV session for KYC, then connects a US bank. Scrub.io reads verified income (Bank Income) and a snapshot of assets and balances (Assets) and produces an eligibility score the home-country lender can trust — turning previously invisible diaspora applicants into underwritable borrowers.
