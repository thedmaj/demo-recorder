================================================================================
Relier — Fund prepaid top-ups with lower ACH risk (Plaid Auth + Balance + Transfer + Signal)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Relier** — diaspora prepaid mobile top-up / bill pay (SMB; US → LatAm recharges)

**User journey (one sentence):**
A US customer topping up a prepaid phone in Latin America connects their bank with Plaid Link, and Relier verifies the account (Auth) and balance, then funds the recharge with Plaid Transfer — where Plaid Signal scores ACH-return risk inside the transfer authorization before the debit clears.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Rosa Méndez sends a $30 mobile top-up to family in Latin America and wants it to land instantly without bounced-payment risk. On Relier she links her US bank in one Plaid Link tap. Relier verifies account/routing (Auth) and available balance, then initiates a Plaid Transfer — and Signal runs inside the transfer authorization to score ACH-return risk on this specific debit. A low-risk authorization clears, and the recharge is funded right away. One connection powers verification, risk, and funding.
