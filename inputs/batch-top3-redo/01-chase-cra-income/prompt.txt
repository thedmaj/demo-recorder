================================================================================

**Host:** **Chase Sapphire** — consumer credit-card application / underwriting
**Canonical URL:** https://www.chase.com
**Brand URL:** https://www.chase.com

**User journey (one sentence):**
A Chase Sapphire applicant reaches the income-verification step, connects their bank through Plaid Link under an FCRA permissible purpose, and Consumer Report by Plaid Check returns report-derived Income Insights that give underwriters bank-connected income context — no PDFs or manual doc chasing.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Income verification for a card application usually means uploading pay stubs, hunting for PDFs, and days of manual review. The applicant instead taps "Verify income with your bank," completes a familiar Plaid Link connection (Consumer Report by Plaid Check, permissible purpose = extension of credit), and the report generates asynchronously. When the report is ready, Income Insights surfaces categorized income streams, historical and forecasted average monthly income, and predicted next-payment timing — a structured, FCRA-aligned input to ability-to-pay review. Close on an underwriting-ready outcome: faster review, fewer follow-ups, decision still under the lender's policy.
