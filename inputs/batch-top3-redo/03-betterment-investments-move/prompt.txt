Betterment — Consolidate Held-Away Investments with Plaid Investments Move
================================================================================

**Host:** **Betterment** — digital wealth / robo-advisor
**Canonical URL:** https://www.betterment.com
**Brand URL:** https://betterment.com

**User journey (one sentence):**
A Betterment customer transfers their held-away IRA from an external brokerage into Betterment in a single guided flow — Plaid's embedded Link widget authenticates the external brokerage and hands Betterment broker-sourced account, ownership, and position data to pre-fill the ACATS submission.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Initiating an ACATS transfer today means hunting for a paper statement, typing an account number, DTC code, and holdings by hand, then waiting days to learn the transfer was rejected for NIGO data (30–50% of ACATS transfers are rejected for mismatched names, wrong account numbers, or bad positions). Plaid Investments Move fixes the inputs: in Betterment's transfer-setup page an embedded Plaid Link widget is mounted inline showing the most-used brokerages; the customer clicks their brokerage tile, authenticates in the modal that surfaces, and approves read-only access. Plaid returns the owner name, full account number, DTC number, and every position — broker-sourced and verified. Betterment pre-fills the ACATS form, shows exactly what will move, and submits. Reveal: 300% more successful ACATS transfers and 90% fewer failures vs. manual entry, with time-to-fund shrinking from weeks of re-submissions to a single guided session.
