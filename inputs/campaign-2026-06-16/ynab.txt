**User journey (one sentence):**
A digital-native young professional links her financial accounts in YNAB with a single
Plaid Link to see spending, investments, and liabilities in one unified money dashboard.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Maya starts with YNAB's core budgeting promise — "give every dollar a job" — but her
spending, brokerage, and mortgage live in separate apps. She connects her accounts once
through Plaid Link, and synced transaction data keeps the budget current without manual
cleanup. From there YNAB expands beyond budgeting into a full financial picture, layering
in investment holdings and mortgage liabilities, and closes with proactive "what to do
next" guidance that makes YNAB feel like a long-term financial command center.

**Product-specific reminders** (delete if N/A):
- Link: exactly one pre-Link host screen and exactly one `plaidPhase: "launch"` step;
  pre-Link narration ends on the **button**, Link step narrates **inside** the modal only.
- Transactions: emphasize merchant name/logo, category (primary + detailed if present),
  amount, date, pending vs posted, and sync updates (added/modified/removed) via
  `/transactions/sync`. No real-time freshness claims beyond what sync supports.
- Investments: read-only HOLDINGS only — `/investments/holdings/get` returns account type,
  ticker symbol, quantity, current price, and market value. **Show NO account numbers / DTC /
  ACATS and NO per-lot tax data** (those are Investments Move, a different product). Use
  product string `investments`, never `investments_auth`.
- Liabilities: emphasize mortgage interest rate, next payment due date, minimum/last payment,
  and last-statement vs current balance. **NO federal student loans** (gone since Aug 2024 /
  Stop Act — Mohela, Nelnet, Aidvantage, EdFinancial, CRI). Mortgage + credit-card data only
  for this demo (private servicers if any student loan appears). Liabilities refreshes ~once
  per day — do not promise live freshness; source "just paid" from Transactions if needed.
- Non-FCRA, non-underwriting framing throughout — this is PFM / financial wellness, not
  credit decisioning. Do not mix `cra_*` products into the Link token.
