Current — Plaid Cash Advance Score (EWA Score) for instant advance underwriting
================================================================================

**Host:** **Current** — consumer neobank / fintech (earned wage access)
**Canonical URL:** https://current.com/
**Brand URL:** https://current.com/

**User journey (one sentence):**
A Current member requests a paycheck advance, and Plaid Cash Advance Score runs at
disbursement decision time via `/signal/evaluate` to instantly approve a right-sized
advance with explainable `core_attributes` and a ruleset decision.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Maya needs cash before payday without a long wait or a credit pull. She opens Current and
taps Get paid early, then links her checking account through Plaid Link. On the advance
review screen, Current calls `/signal/evaluate` and reads `scores.cash_advance.score = 27`
(1–99, lower = safer to approve). Two named `core_attributes` (stable inflows, low cross-EWA
churn) plus a ruleset rule fire — `ruleset.result: ACCEPT` — and her $150 advance is approved
and disbursed instantly into her Current balance. A closing beat ties the network +
cash-flow score to fewer defaults and safer, right-sized approvals across EWA cohorts.

**Product-specific reminders:**
- Cash Advance Score: score is `scores.cash_advance.score`, range 1–99, **higher = higher risk**;
  approve at LOW scores (typical EWA approve threshold ≤ 30). Anchor the reveal to the documented
  `ruleset.result` value **`ACCEPT`** (NOT `APPROVE` / `REJECT` — those are not documented Signal
  values) plus 2–3 named `core_attributes`. Use repayment / EWA-disbursement language, not ACH
  return-risk wording. Do NOT invent a top-level `reason_codes[]` array.
- Link: the pre-Link step (beat 2) ends on the button ("…she taps Link your bank."); the Link step
  (beat 3) narrates content visible INSIDE the modal only — never "Plaid Link opens."
