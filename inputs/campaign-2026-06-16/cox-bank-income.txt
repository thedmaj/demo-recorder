================================================================================

**Host:** **Cox Automotive** — automotive retail / indirect lending (dealer finance desk)
**Canonical URL:** https://www.coxautoinc.com/
**Brand URL:** https://www.coxautoinc.com/

**User journey (one sentence):**
A dealer finance manager verifies a buyer's income by having them link their checking
account through Plaid Link — replacing paystub uploads — and Plaid Bank Income returns
verified income streams that clear the buyer for auto financing in seconds.

**Story arc (short prose):**
At the dealership finance desk, verifying a buyer's income usually means chasing
paystubs, W-2s, or bank statements — slow, fraud-prone, and a drop-off point in the
deal. The finance manager offers the buyer a faster path: connect your bank securely
with Plaid. The buyer taps Link your bank on the pre-Link screen, completes Plaid Link,
and Plaid Bank Income returns verified income — primary employer, monthly income, and a
consistent deposit history. The finance desk sees confirmed income and clears the buyer
for financing, closing the deal without a single document upload. Close on faster
funding decisions, fewer stips, and less income-fraud exposure.

**CRITICAL — Plaid Bank Income, NOT CRA Income Insights:** This is **traditional Plaid
Bank Income** (`income_verification`), NOT Plaid Check / Consumer Report / CRA
(`cra_base_report`, `cra_income_insights`, LendScore). Retrieval endpoint is
`POST /credit/bank_income/get` (requires a `user_token` from `/user/create`). An
`income_verification` Link token accepts ONLY `{income_verification}` or
`{income_verification, employment}` — do NOT bundle `auth`, `identity`, `signal`,
`transactions`, or `cra_*` into the same Link token. `products[]` is research-driven; use
the resolver's `suggestedClientRequest` verbatim. Bank Income is non-FCRA — keep claims
income-awareness/verification framed (not a regulated credit decision).
