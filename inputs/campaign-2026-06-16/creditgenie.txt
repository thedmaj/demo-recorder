================================================================================
Credit Genie — One-tap debt-relief onboarding to a cash-flow-sized plan
(Plaid Layer + Plaid Consumer Report: CRA Base Report + CRA Cashflow Insights)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Credit Genie** — AI-driven debt-relief and financial-health app (consumer)
**Canonical URL:** https://www.creditgenie.com
**Brand URL:** https://www.creditgenie.com

**User journey (one sentence):**
A consumer seeking debt relief taps once to onboard with Plaid Layer — a single session that shares his prefilled identity and permissions his bank — and Credit Genie generates a Plaid Consumer Report server-side to size a personalized debt-relief plan on 365 days of verified cash flow plus CRA Cashflow Insights.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Marcus Bell is juggling several payments and wants a realistic plan, fast. Credit Genie's signup loses people to long forms, so it leads with Plaid Layer: in one tap Marcus reviews his prefilled name, email, phone, and address AND permissions his bank — a single Layer session, no second connection step. Credit Genie writes that identity to the Plaid user record and generates his Consumer Report server-side. It then reads the **CRA Base Report** (365-day cash flow — inflows, outflows, stable balances, zero NSFs) and **CRA Cashflow Insights** (income stream, recurring obligations, free cash flow) to size a debt-relief plan that fits what Marcus can actually afford. Compliance-safe language only ("can help," "typically," "up to").

**Product-specific reminders:**
- CRA: ONE launch only (Layer). The report-generation beat (step 3) is a server-side async state, NOT a `plaidPhase` launch. Async report-ready beat is present (step 3 → step 4).
- Layer: pre-Layer step (1) narration ends on the **button** ("…he taps Continue with Plaid."); the Layer step (2) narrates content **inside** the modal only.
- Layer identity is user-permissioned; written to the Plaid user record via `/user/update` before report generation. Frame as "shared / permissioned," not document-verified KYC.
- CRA Base Report / CRA Cashflow Insights are FCRA consumer-report products — compliance-safe, "can help" / "typically" framing; happy path only. Cashflow Insights retrieved via `/cra/check_report/cashflow_insights/get`, NOT `/credit/bank_income/get`. CRA Cashflow Insights is beta — note beta status if production rollout is discussed.
- Keep webhook/event names (`USER_CHECK_REPORT_READY`, `SESSION_FINISHED`), raw endpoints, raw enums, and `user_id`/`report_id` OFF host/consumer screens — they live in the `#api-response-panel` (raw JSON) or a slide only.
