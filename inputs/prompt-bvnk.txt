**Solution Fit (from Gong call "Plaid + BVNK | Solutions Overview + Demo", 2026-04-29):**
- Plaid positioned as the US ACH journey: account linking, account verification, and fraud
  controls for embedded-partner flows.
- Reseller model with distinct end-client IDs is the cleanest scalable approach for BVNK's
  B2B2C setup; embedded partners get fully wrapped Plaid Link UX with their own branding.
- Perceived benefits BVNK called out: embedded-partner branding flexibility, traffic
  segregation by end-client ID, low-friction wrapped partner experience, optional headless
  front end, **Identity Match plus Signal for verification and ACH risk checks**.

**Business objectives (derived from Salesforce + Gong):**
- Give BVNK's embedded partners a turnkey US ACH on-ramp into BVNK virtual accounts.
- Reduce drop-off on first-time funding by replacing manual account-and-routing entry and
  micro-deposits with credential-based bank linking.
- Confirm account ownership before moving money (Identity Match for B2C and SMB users).
- Make fast funding decisions with real-time ACH return-risk scoring (Signal) so good
  users get instant credit and risky funding gets stepped up — without exposing BVNK or
  its embedded partners to ACH return loss.

**User journey (one sentence):**
A US small-business owner using a BVNK-powered virtual account links her business
checking with Plaid, gets verified for ownership, receives a low-risk ACH decision, and
funds her account instantly to settle a stablecoin payout to an overseas supplier.

**Story arc (short prose):**
BVNK's embedded partner needs a US ACH on-ramp into a BVNK USD virtual account. The
partner brand is in front of the customer; Plaid runs invisibly underneath via BVNK's
reseller integration. The customer connects her bank in one Plaid Link session, BVNK
sees verified routing and account numbers, an Identity Match score that confirms she
owns the linked account, and a low Signal ACH return-risk score that authorizes
instant funding — turning a multi-day funding step into seconds without giving up
fraud and risk controls.

INFERRED API STACK (IMPLEMENTATION INTENT)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Use these endpoints and sequencing for this use case:
1. `POST /link/token/create` (products: `auth`; optional `identity_match` request via
   token config or downstream `/identity/match` call)
2. Plaid Link `onSuccess` → token exchange path
3. `POST /auth/get`
4. `POST /identity/match` (business-name and owner-name match dimensions)
5. `POST /signal/evaluate` (score + decision + top risk attributes)
6. `POST /signal/decision/report` (optional follow-up; not required to render in host UI)

API panel expectations:
- Show request and response samples that support each beat's claim.
- For `/identity/match`, include separate `business_name_match` and `owner_name_match`
  fields so the business-vs-owner distinction is visible on screen.
- For `/signal/evaluate`, surface the chosen score, decision, and 2–3 top risk
  attributes — keep payload realistic, do not pad with every available field.
