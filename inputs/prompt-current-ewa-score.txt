**User journey (one sentence):**
A Current member requests a paycheck advance; Plaid Cash Advance Score runs at decision time via `/signal/evaluate` and instantly approves a right-sized amount with explainable ruleset rules + core_attributes.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Maya opens Current and taps Get paid early. She confirms her linked checking account. On the advance review screen, Current calls `/signal/evaluate` and reads `scores.cash_advance.score = 27` (low = good). Two named core_attributes (stable inflows, low cross-EWA churn) plus a ruleset rule fire — **Approved $150**. Funds land in her Current balance. A closing beat ties the score to fewer defaults and higher safe approval rates for EWA cohorts.
