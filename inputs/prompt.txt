TD Bank (US) Account Funding — Ownership-Verified Instant Pay by Bank
================================================================================

## Objective
Create a production-grade, hyper-realistic TD funding journey where a customer links an external account, verifies ownership, enters a funding amount, and receives an instant low-risk Pay by Bank approval.

Prioritize visual credibility and authentic UX over rigid storyboard literalism while preserving product truth and compliance constraints.

## Core Context
- **Host:** TD Bank (United States) — retail banking / digital servicing
- **Canonical URL:** https://www.td.com
- **Brand URL:** https://www.td.com
- **Primary product family:** `funding`
- **Products featured:** Plaid Link, Plaid Identity Match, Plaid Auth, Plaid Signal
- **Solutions supported:** Funding, Account Opening, Fraud & Risk Controls
- **Primary messaging file:** `inputs/plaid-value-props.md`
- **Research depth:** messaging

## Non-Negotiables
- Use live Plaid Link and real happy-path flow capture.
- Keep product naming and semantics accurate (approved product names only).
- Signal interpretation must remain correct: lower score = lower ACH return risk; happy path = low score + `ACCEPT`.
- Show a clear business outcome at the end (instant, trusted funding completion).
- Use TD-like consumer banking chrome for host steps; keep Plaid insight steps visually distinct.
- Design for 1440x900.

## Creative Freedom (Explicit)
- Choose the strongest UX structure, spacing, and interaction hierarchy for realism.
- You may merge/split host moments as needed if business logic is preserved.
- Prefer natural product UX patterns (information architecture, progressive disclosure, meaningful affordances) over checklist-like UI.
- Avoid generic template aesthetics; make it feel like a real TD production surface.

## Scene Goals (Outcome-Based, Ordered)
1. Establish TD funding context and user motivation to add an external account.
2. Build trust before handoff (security + speed + clarity of next action).
3. Show Plaid Link completion as the connection moment.
4. Confirm ownership and account readiness using Identity Match + Auth insights.
5. Capture funding amount and run Signal decisioning for that exact transaction.
6. Reveal low-risk `ACCEPT` and instant funding confirmation with quantified value.

## Persona and Required On-Screen Data
- **Persona:** Maya Patel, TD retail customer
- **Context:** Funding a TD checking account from an external account for an upcoming bill
- **Must-show fields:** external account mask, funding amount, ownership status, routing/account readiness, Signal score + `ACCEPT`

## Evidence Anchors (Externally Safe Ranges)
- Funded account conversion lift: **+10% to +25%**
- Time to first fund: **2x to 8x faster**
- Funding-step drop-off reduction: **15% to 30% lower**
- ACH risk outcome improvement: **30% to 50% fewer ACH return / unauthorized outcomes**
- Funding cost-to-serve reduction: **10% to 20% lower**
- Funding-related support contact reduction: **20% to 40% fewer**

Use range-based language (“can help,” “supports,” “observed range”). Avoid deterministic guarantees.

## Constraints
- Frame outputs as decision support for account funding and risk controls; do not claim guaranteed approvals.
- Keep one coherent stats source and avoid conflicting claims.
