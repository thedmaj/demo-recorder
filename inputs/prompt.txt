HOST & PROBLEM
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Host: **MyKidsSpending** (Odin, Inc.) — K–12 school spending & tuition funding portal used by independent schools and families.

User journey (one sentence):
A parent signs into their school-branded MyKidsSpending dashboard, replaces clunky e-check and stored-bank-detail flows with a guided bank connection, verifies identity for ownership, and sees risk-aware settlement readiness so schools get paid with fewer ACH returns.

Story arc (short prose):
Families and schools lose time to manual bank verification and returned ACH debits. Odin is modernizing funding by routing parents through **modal Plaid Link** for **Plaid Instant Auth** (verified account and routing numbers), **Plaid Identity Verification (IDV)** with a clear manual-entry fallback path when automated verification cannot complete in one pass, **Plaid Assets** for real-time balance context that informs payout timing, and **Plaid Signal** for ACH risk scoring so settlement policies favor low-return, faster-money paths to schools. The demo ends with an ACCEPT-grade Signal outcome and a clear “ready to fund” state; narration may mention that Odin can later adopt **Plaid Transfer** for end-to-end ACH orchestration (future-looking beat only — no Transfer UI in scope).
