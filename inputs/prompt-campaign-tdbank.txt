================================================================================
TD Bank — Instant External Account Funding — Fund a TD account from any bank in seconds, with ownership + ACH-risk checks built in
================================================================================

**Host:** **TD Bank** — U.S. retail banking (consumer deposit account funding)
**Canonical URL:** https://www.td.com
**Brand URL:** https://www.td.com

**User journey (one sentence):**
A new TD customer funds their TD checking account from an external bank by connecting it with Plaid, and TD instantly verifies the account belongs to them, pulls the ACH rails, and clears the transfer for ACH-return risk before approving the funds.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Opening a deposit account is easy — funding it is where customers stall on slow micro-deposits and where fraud and ACH returns creep in. TD drops in Plaid: the customer links their external bank in one tap, and behind the scenes TD confirms the accountholder matches its KYC (Identity Match), pulls account + routing rails (Auth), captures the amount, and risk-scores that specific transfer (Signal). Low risk → funds approved instantly, no micro-deposits. Close on the value: faster funded accounts, fewer returns, less fraud.

**Product-specific reminders:**
- Signal: low score = low ACH return risk; happy path **ACCEPT** with low scores (customer-initiated 12, bank-initiated 10). `ruleset.result` in {ACCEPT, REVIEW, REROUTE} — happy path ACCEPT.
- Identity Match: scores 0–100; name/address/phone strong, email weak to show nuance. Never label scores "Trust Index".
- Link: the pre-Link step ends on the **button** ("…taps Link an external bank."); the Link step narrates content **inside** the modal only.
- Consumer TD screens never show raw JSON; the JSON belongs only in the global `#api-response-panel`.
