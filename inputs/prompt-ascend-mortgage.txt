Ascend Bank — Verify mortgage income & assets in minutes (Plaid Consumer Report — CRA Base Report + CRA Income Insights)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Ascend Bank** — digital mortgage lender (home-loan origination)
(Branding is supplied locally via inputs/brand-references/ascend-bank.md + inputs/brand-assets/ascend-bank-logo.png — do NOT fetch external branding. No Brand URL.)

**User journey (one sentence):**
A borrower applying for a home loan on Ascend Bank connects their bank once through Plaid Link with FCRA-permissible consent, and Ascend verifies income (CRA Income Insights) and assets + cash flow (CRA Base Report) from a Plaid Consumer Report to issue a verified mortgage pre-approval — instead of weeks of uploading bank statements and pay stubs.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Daniel Reyes is applying for a $480,000 home loan. The slowest, most painful part of his mortgage application is the document chase: months of bank statements, pay stubs, and asset letters, manually uploaded and manually reviewed. Ascend Bank replaces that with one Plaid Link connection. Daniel grants FCRA-permissible consent and connects his bank; Plaid generates a Consumer Report asynchronously. Ascend reads the CRA Base Report (90-day cash flow, account balances for assets-to-close, zero NSFs) and CRA Income Insights (verified employer, biweekly payroll, monthly net income, predicted next payday), then issues a verified pre-approval with a clear assets + income basis — in minutes, not weeks. Compliance-safe language only ("can help," "typically," "up to"; no guarantees).
