**Business objectives (derived from Salesforce + Plaid Bank Income product):**
- Replace manual paystub / W-2 upload with instant bank-derived income verification.
- Compress time-to-decision for the captive auto program (BofA finances Lucid vehicles directly through its dealership network).
- Reduce stipulation rate and the "abandoned at upload" drop-off that costs lenders measurable conversion in auto.
- Confirm applicant identity matches the linked bank account before approving the loan.

**User journey (one sentence):**
A Lucid buyer applies for a BofA auto loan through the dealership portal, links his payroll bank with Plaid to verify income in seconds, and receives an approved offer with rate and term — no paystubs, no W-2s, no email back-and-forth.

**Story arc (short prose):**
A Lucid customer at a Bank of America-financed dealership starts a credit application. Instead of uploading paystubs, he taps "Verify income with your bank" and connects his payroll bank through Plaid Link. Plaid Bank Income returns a structured income summary (recurring deposits, employer, monthly amount, stability metrics); Plaid Identity Match confirms the bank account is his. BofA's underwriter rule engine ingests the structured income, prices the loan, and the customer sees an approved offer with monthly payment in under a minute — turning a multi-day stipulation cycle into an in-session decision.

INFERRED API STACK (IMPLEMENTATION INTENT)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Endpoints and sequencing for this use case:
1. `POST /link/token/create` (products: `bank_income`, `identity` / `identity_match`)
2. Plaid Link `onSuccess` → token exchange path (`/item/public_token/exchange`)
3. `POST /credit/bank_income/get` — structured income with `historical_summary`, `pay_frequency`, top employer
4. `POST /identity/match` — legal_name / address / phone / email match scores
5. (Display) lender decisioning step — host renders APPROVED / APR / payment

API panel expectations:
- Panel JSON for beats 5 and 6 must align with the on-screen claim (employer + monthly income visible on Bank Income beat; per-attribute name/address/phone/email scores on Identity Match beat).
- Keep payloads realistic and lean — do not pad with every available field.
