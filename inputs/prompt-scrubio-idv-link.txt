================================================================================
Scrub.io — Underwrite diaspora borrowers on US bank data (Identity Verification + CRA Consumer Report)
================================================================================

**Host:** **Scrub.io** — cross-border lending infrastructure (SMB; underwriting data provider)

**User journey (one sentence):**
A diaspora applicant clears a live Plaid Identity Verification session, then links a US bank through a Plaid Check Consumer Report Link so Scrub.io can pull CRA Income Insights and a CRA Base Report (verified income + balances/cash flow) to score loan eligibility for a lender in the applicant's home country.

**Story arc (short prose):**
Chidi Okafor lives in the US and is applying for a loan from a Nigerian lender that has no visibility into US financial data. On Scrub.io, Chidi completes Plaid Identity Verification for KYC — a real IDV session with phone, date-of-birth, and SMS verification — then connects a US bank through a Plaid Check Consumer Report Link. Scrub.io generates a consumer report server-side: CRA Income Insights confirm a regular income stream and the CRA Base Report returns 365 days of balances and cash flow. The result is an FCRA-grade eligibility score the home-country lender can trust — turning previously invisible diaspora applicants into underwritable borrowers.

  products (no auth/identity/assets/transactions in the same token).
